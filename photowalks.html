Learn architectural photography with us

Before you start your photographic adventure with us, tell us something about yourself

Our photo workshops are available to everyone. From amateur to advanced hobbyist. We also run groups for professional photographers where technical knowledge is limited to bare minium but we will walk you around the city and show the most beautifully ugly architecture and talk about history of those mouth-watering construction. Workshops for professionals are run by our friendly architect who will take you inside the building to places normally not accessible to the public. We usually conduct such classes only in the summer time in the late afternoon hours and in the early morning hours on Sundays. Our workshops are usaully not weather permiting. We even take pictures when it's raining and the wind is blowing off our heads - so don't forget to bring a sturdy umbrella. Only an earthquake or an invasion of aliens can stop us ;)
